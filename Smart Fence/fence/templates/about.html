ABOUT:

The aim of this project is to develop an IoT-based border defense mechanism that can provide real-time surveillance, threat detection, and automated response capabilities. The proposed system will consist of a network of sensors, cameras, and other IoT devices that will be integrated with advanced analytics software to provide actionable intelligence to border security personnel.
